Summary: Latitude vs Wind Speed

Analysis of weather information for 547 cities around the world was carried out. Our goal was to understand impact of city's proximity to the equator on its weather.

We can observe that maximum wind speeds in the Northern hemisphere tend to be higher than those in the Southern Hemisphere.

Wind speeds increase away from the equator in the Northern hemisphere much more quickly than in the South, where only a mild increase can be seen

More samples are available from the Northern Hemisphere.
